HTML이란 무엇인가?

Hypertext Markup Language (HTML) is a code used to structure web pages and their contents. For example, content can be structured with multiple paragraphs, bullet lists, or a picture or data table. Like the title, this article will provide a basic understanding of HTML and its features.

HTML is a markup language that defines the structure of content. HTML consists of a series of elements that cover or enclose different parts of content to look different or to behave in a particular way. Tag-wrapping allows you to hyperlink words or images to somewhere else, italicize words, make the font larger or smaller, and so on
